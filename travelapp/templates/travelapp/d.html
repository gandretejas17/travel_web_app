Gangasagar/Jagannathpuri/Nepal Yatra

Pilgrimage Places & Sightseeing


  1. Starts from Karanja Lad with Guru Mauli Darshan
  2. Ramtek : Shri Ram Temple & Other Temples
  3. Dongargarh : Shri Bamleshwari Devi Darshan, Electric Cradle
  4. Hirakud : Longest Dam on Mahanadi (24kms)
  5. Sambalpur : Sambalpur Mata Darshan
  6. Bhuvaneshwar : Capital of Orissa, Svayambhu Lingaraj Temple Darshan
  7. Konark : A World Famous Piece of Sculpture
  8. Shri Kshetra Puri : Hindu's Holy Place, Lovely Beach View & Other Temples
  9. SakshiGopal : Lord Shri Krishna Temple Darshan
  10. Kolkata : Kalimata Darshan, Victoria Memorial, Zoo
  11. Belurmath : Swami Vivekananda's Cloister at Ganga River Bank
  12. Dakshineshwar : Ramkrishna Paramhans Cloister (Kalanka Temple)
  13. Shri Kshetra Gangasagar : Ganga & Sagar Sangam Snan, Kapil Muni Darsh Mahasagar Mela
  14. Sitamadhi : Birthplace of Sita
  15. Janakpur : Janakpuri King Janak's Palace
  16. Kathmandu (Nepal) : Pashupatinath Darshan
  17. Shri Kshetra Gaya : Pitrutirtha Pinddan Area on River Phalgu, Vishnupad Vihar
  18. Buddha Gaya : Buddha Stupa, Bodhi Vruksh & Other Buddha Vihar(Temple)
  19. Kashi : Varanasi-Banaras-Hindus's Holy Place Gorakhpur at Ganga River (Gorakhnath Temple)
  20. Ayodhya : Birthplace of Lord Shri Ram, Hanuman Gadhi, Kanak Bhavan
  21. Prayagraj : Ganga-Yamuna- Saraswati Sangam, Akshayvat, Anandbhavan
  22. Chitrakoot : Ramghat, Tulsidas Ashram, Ram-Bharat Bhet, Sati Anusaya, Gupt Godawari Temple
  23. Maiyar : Sharada Devi Darshan
  24. Bhedaghat : Dhuandhar Falls
