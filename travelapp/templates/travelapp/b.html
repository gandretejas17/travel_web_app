Dwarka/Sorti-Somnath/Rajasthan/Gujarat Yatra

Pilgrimage Places & Sightseeing


  1. Starts from Karanja Lad with Guru Mauli Darshan
  2. Shegaon : Shri Gajanan Maharaj Darshan
  3. Omkareshwar : Omkareshwar Jyotirling Darshan
  4. Ujjain : Mahakaleshwar Jyotirling, Kalbhairav Darshan Dham
  5. Mandsaur : Pashupatinath Temple
  6. Chittor : A Historic Fort
  7. Udaipur : Maharana Pratap's Historic Place
  8. Eklingi : Maharana Pratap's Kuladevata
  9. Nathdwara : Shri Nathji Darshan
  10. Jaipur : Pink City of India,Jantar-Mantar Amar Fort
  11. Pushkar : Bramha Temple,Pushkar Talav
  12. Merta : Sant Mirabai Birthplace
  13. Deshnokh : Karnika Mata Temple (The Famous Rat Temple)
  14. Ramdevara : Sant Ramdevbaba Darshan
  15. Mount Abu : Nakki Jheel, Dilwada Temple,Prajapati Bramhakumari Temple,Hill Station
  16. Ambaji : Ambaji Darshan
  17. Siddhapur : Matrugaya
  18. Gandhinagar : Akshardham, Swami Narayan Temple
  19. Dwarka : Amongs 4-Dham,Gomati Snan,Capital of Lord Shri Krishna
  20. Bet Dwarka : Dwarkadhish Temple (beyond Sea)
  21. Gopi Talav : Sacrifice Place of Gopis
  22. Nageshwar : Nageshwar Jyotirling Darshan
  23. Sudagpuri : Bhakt Sudam Nagari
  24. Porbandar : Birthplace of Gandhi
  25. Sorti-Somnath : Sorti-Somnath Jyotirling Darshan
  26. Prabhat Paatan : Lord Shri Krishna Voluntary Death Place
  27. Girnar : Navnath Shikhar at Giri Mountain
  28. Junagarh : Sant Narsinha Mehta Darshan
  29. Virpur : Sant Jalaram Baba Darshan
  30. Dakorji : Shri Krishna Darshan, Dakorji Darshan
  31. Sardar Sarovar : Statue of Unity
  32. Adilabad : Mira Temple
