Vaishnodevi/Badrinath/Kedarnath/Gangotri/Yamunotri Yatra

Pilgrimage Places & Sightseeing


  1. Starts from Karanja Lad with Guru Mauli Darshan
  2. Shegaon : Shri Sant Gajanan Maharaj Darshan
  3. Omkareshwar : Narmada Snan, Omkareshwar Jyotirling Darshan, Mamleshwar Darshan
  4. Ujjain : Shripra Snan,Ramghat,Harsiddhi Devi Darshan,Bada Ganapati,Bhartari Cave,Mangal Dham,Kalbhairav Darshan
  5. Agra : Famous Taj Mahal,Dayalab
  6. Mathura : Homeland of ShrikRishna,RadhaKrishna Temple
  7. Vrindavan : Rangnath Temple,Bhajanashram,Kunjgali,Kaliya Mardan,Gopi Vastraharan
  8. Gokul : Yamuna Ghat Nandanvan
  9. Delhi : Capital of India,Kutub Minar,Lotus Temple,Birla Temple,Indira Gandhi Smriti Temple,Rashtrapati Bhavan,Vidhansabha,India Gate,26-January Road(Bramhadarshan),Rajghat,Laal Kila
  10. Kurukshetra : Kaurav-Pandav Battlefield,Gitasar,DynanYagya,Suryakund
  11. Amritsar : Golden Temple,Jaliyanwala Baug
  12. Wagha : Ind-Pak Border
  13. Katra : Depart for Vaishnodevi
  14. Vaishnodevi : 14 kms Vaishnodevi Darshan
  15. Jalandhar : Devi Talav Darshan
  16. Haridwar : Gangasnan, Harki Paundi Darshan,Manasadevi(Ropeway)
  17. Rishikesh : Gangasnan, Rishikund Darshan
  18. Gangotri : Origin of Ganga River(Gangotri Temple)
  19. Yamunotri : Origin of Yamuna River(Yamunotri Temple)
  20. Gaurikund : Hot Water Kund,Paravati's Penance Place
  21. Kedarnath : Kedarnath Jyotirling (14 kms by walk,dindi,kandi,horseride,etc.)
  22. Badrinath : Hindu's Pavitra Dham (Main amongs 4-Dham), Hot Water Kund
  23. Sukratal : King Parikshit's Bhagawat Shravan Place
  24. Betul : Birla Temple, Balaji Temple
